Image Registries

A common lament in data science is that most of a researcher’s time is spent organizing and cleaning data for further analysis. OSAIL has invested heavily in the creation of large-scale imaging registries with images pulled from Mayo Clinic’s electronic health record system and automatically labeled for future use with bespoke annotation algorithms. These registries allow for rapid project progression and large cohort studies which would be otherwise prohibitively laborious.
