Bardia Khosravi MD MPH MHPE

Data Scientist

Bardia Khosravi is a MD, with two masters in medical education (MHPE) and public health (MPH), from Tehran University of Medical Sciences (TUMS). He has been a full stack developer since he was 19. Since the pandemic, he started focusing his programming skills on deep learning as way of helping those suffering with the disease, through early diagnosis and efficient triage. He started his research at RIL in 2021 and has been working on complications’ risk prediction in orthopedic patients, and other generative tasks. Bardia’s main hobby is cooking, especially Italian food, with some Persian touch!
