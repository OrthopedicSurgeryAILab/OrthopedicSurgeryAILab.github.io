Michael Taunton MD

Director, Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Taunton is a Professor of Orthopedic Surgery at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester MN. He is the program director of the Hip and Knee Reconstruction fellowship at Mayo Clinic. Dr. Taunton is also the Co-director of the Orthopedic Surgery Artificial Intelligence Lab at Mayo Clinic. He is a fellowship trained orthopedic surgeon in hip and knee reconstruction. His special interests are the direct anterior approach for total hip arthroplasty, advanced pelvic and femoral reconstruction for revision total hip arthroplasty, as well as knee arthroplasty, and machine learning algorithms applied to the arthroplasty practice.

Dr. Taunton hails from southern Missouri and completed medical school at St. Louis University School of Medicine, in St. Louis Missouri. His orthopedic residency was performed at the Mayo Clinic in Rochester, MN, and an adult reconstruction fellowship at Orthocarolina in Charlotte, NC.
