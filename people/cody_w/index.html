Cody Wyles MD

Director, Orthopedic Surgeon

Dr. Wyles holds dual appointments as an Assistant Professor of Orthopedic Surgery and Clinical Anatomy and has a surgical practice focused on complex hip and knee reconstruction at Mayo Clinic in Rochester, Minnesota. He has authored 150 peer-reviewed publications, 4 Hip Society and Knee Society Awards, CORR® ORS Richard A. Brand Award for Outstanding Orthopaedic Research, and several other notable awards in orthopedic research. His primary research interest is leveraging the tools of AI to enhance patient-specific phenotyping and assess the natural history of degenerative hip and knee diseases for improved predictive analytics.
